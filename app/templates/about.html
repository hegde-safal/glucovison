About GlucoVision

Empowering you with precision nutrition intelligence for better metabolic health.

Our Mission

GlucoVision transforms complex nutritional data into clear, actionable insights. We bridge the gap between simple calorie counting and clinical-grade metabolic analysis, helping you make informed decisions for long-term health.

Advanced AI

Powered by state-of-the-art NLP and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), our engine understands your natural language logs ("oatmeal with berries") and matches them against a validated database of 6,000+ foods to calculate precise glycaemic loads.

Privacy First

Your health data is personal. GlucoVision stores your logs and settings locally on your device or secure local database. We believe in providing powerful insights without compromising your digital privacy.

How It Works

1

2

3

Medical Disclaimer

GlucoVision provides dietary analysis based on standard food databases. It is not a diagnostic tool and does not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with a healthcare provider for managing specific conditions.
